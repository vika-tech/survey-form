Isaac Newton

Isaac Newton, an English physicist and mathematician, is celebrated for his groundbreaking contributions to science, particularly his laws of motion and universal gravitation, which laid the foundation for classical mechanics. He also made significant advancements in optics and mathematics, including the development of calculus. His work profoundly impacted the Scientific Revolution and continues to influence scientific thought.
